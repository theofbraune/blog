About me

I am a PhD student in the Geomerix group at LIX in École Polytechnique under the supervision of Mathieu Desbrun.

In my work I focus on computational differential geometry, especially discrete exterior calculus. We work on extensions to discrete vector bundle valued forms and its possible applications.

Education

  • M2 in Computer Science at IP-Paris
  • Master in Mathematics at TU Berlin
  • M1 in Mathematics at École Normale Superieure
  • Bachelor in Mathematics at TU Berlin
