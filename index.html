Hi, I'm Theo Braune

PhD Student in Computer Science in IP-Paris/ École Polytechnique/ Inria

In my work I focus on computational differential geometry, especially discrete exterior calculus. We work on extensions to discrete vector bundle valued forms and its possible applications.

Latest News

  • We put our article about discrete exterior calculus for bundle valued forms in the Arxiv. Check it out here.

    In this work we present a structure preserving discrete description of bundle valued exterior calculus. We introduce a discrete covariant exterior derivative that satisfies the Bianchi Identities and converges in the limit under mesh refinement.

  • I started my PhD position in October 2022

    I work under the supervision of Mathieu Desbrun in close collaboration with Yiying Tong from MSU and François Gay-Balmaz from NTU on extensions for discrete exterior calculus and possible applications.
