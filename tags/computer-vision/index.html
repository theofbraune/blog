Heart Rate Measurement through Computer Vision

Posted by: Theo and Jingyi Mar 30, 2022, 1:15PM programming projects Computer Vision
For many applications in telemedicine or in surveillance a contactless heart rate measurement can be extremely useful. Most of the conventional approaches use a wearable object like a chest belt or a special watch. In the mentioned cases, this is not applicable. Therefore one is interested in methods, that can extract the heart rate from a video feed.
