Implementation of Ski Locomotion in Virtual Reality.

Posted by: Theo Jan 31, 2022, 2:29PM virtual reality programming projects
In my locomotion project I went for an approach to imitate cross-country-skiing in VR. The overall idea will be, that you have to move your arms from top to down, and this then gives you a push.
